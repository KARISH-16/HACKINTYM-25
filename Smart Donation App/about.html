About Us

Smart Donation Map is a tech-powered platform connecting donors, NGOs, and volunteers in real time.

Our Mission

To reduce waste and hunger by mapping donations and enabling instant pickups.

Our Vision

A hunger-free world where surplus food and essentials reach those who need them — fast, smart, and sustainably.

What Makes Us Different

  • Real-time map pins for donations
  • SMS access for low-tech users
  • Volunteer-powered delivery network
  • Transparent impact tracking
