Become a Volunteer

Join our network of everyday heroes who help bridge the gap between surplus and need. Volunteers are the heartbeat of our mission.

What You Can Do:

  • Pick up food or essentials from donors
  • Deliver to mapped NGOs or shelters
  • Get real-time alerts of nearby needs
  • Track your impact and hours

Why It Matters:

Every pickup you complete feeds someone. Every hour you contribute builds a more compassionate world.
