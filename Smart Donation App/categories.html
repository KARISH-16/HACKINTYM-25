Food Donations

Whether it’s leftover food from a wedding, birthday, or restaurant, you can mark it on the map as Available Now. Our volunteers ensure timely pickup and safe delivery to shelters and NGOs.

  • Fresh cooked meals
  • Packaged food with valid expiry
  • Fruits, vegetables, grains

Money Donations

Your contributions help fund food transport, cold storage, and logistics. 100% of donations go to verified NGOs and community kitchens.

Clothes Donations

Drop-off centers are mapped for easy access. We accept gently used clothes, school uniforms, and winter wear.

Essentials

Books, blankets, shoes, hygiene kits — all mapped for pickup or drop-off. These items go to orphanages, shelters, and relief camps.
